About This Event

The CCS Stakeholders' Forum serves as a critical platform for open dialogue between the College of Computer Studies and its external partners. Through structured presentations, panel discussions, and feedback sessions, the forum aims to align CCS academic programs with the evolving needs of industry and society. Stakeholders will provide insights on current technology trends, workforce requirements, and how CCS graduates can be better prepared for the professional landscape.

Key Activities
  • Stakeholder presentations and panel discussions on the relevance of CCS programs to industry needs
  • Structured feedback gathering on curriculum alignment with current and emerging technology demands
  • Exploration of strategic partnership and collaboration opportunities between CCS and the ICT sector
  • Open forum for recommendations on graduate competencies and employability
Participants
CCS Faculty Members, Industry Partners, Government Agency Representatives, Alumni Leaders, and Other Stakeholders
