What Is It

A Week of Academic Excellence & Institutional Growth

CCS Future-Ready Week 2026 is a series of graduate activities organized by the College of Computer Studies at MSU-IIT. Authorized by the Office of the University Chancellor upon the recommendation of the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs, the week brings together faculty, stakeholders, alumni, and academic leaders to assess, plan, and strengthen the college's programs and research directions.

Objectives

Shaping the Future of CCS

The week's activities are designed to:

  • Engage stakeholders in the direction and quality of CCS programs
  • Foster a strong research culture and uphold research ethics
  • Assess college sustainability and institutional performance
  • Reconnect with alumni through graduate tracing and engagement
  • Conduct curriculum quality improvement and materials development

Authorized by the Office of the University Chancellor
